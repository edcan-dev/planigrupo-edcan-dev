    Link with the Community

    In the case of community engagement, we get involved in projects and collaborations that They contribute to the sustainable development of the communities in which we operate. From volunteer programs to infrastructure improvement projects and support for social and environmental causes, we work in close collaboration with communities to generate a positive and lasting impact.


    Our commitment to the community has sometimes allowed us to be a bridge between the entities governments and civil associations that seek to promote or provide resources or activities, thereby we have generated important synergies within the entities in which our places are located commercial.

    "Banco de Tapitas", which helps pay for chemotherapy for children with cancer. During the year, We collected 39.5 tons of caps, equivalent to 745 chemotherapies or 1,495 doses of nutritional supplements.

    Together with Grupo Allen, we recycled 30 tons of PET plastic, which is equivalent to 52,460 Kg of C02, that were no longer emitted into the environment. In addition, we join the initiative.

    "The Largest School in the World" to promote education and provide safe spaces in our Places for students. In 8 Commercial Plazas, a space was adapted in the Food Court area so that children and young students have access to the internet through our Wi-Fi and can continue with their studies.

    Where we are present, we support other initiatives such as the Telethon through CRIT Coahuila. We were also recognized as a "Compassionate Company" in Nuevo León and in various recycling collections in Cancun, among other activities.

    Diversity and inclusion

Also, the “ANTICANCER ALLIANCE” where an activity to collect PET toys and face masks to support this Foundation.
