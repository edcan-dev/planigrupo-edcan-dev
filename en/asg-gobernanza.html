    Governance

    At GM Inmobiliaria we understand that solid governance is essential to ensure proper functioning efficient and ethical of our organization. Throughout our history, we have distinguished ourselves by being leaders in the adoption and execution of the best corporate governance practices with the purpose of generate value sustainably through the company, prioritizing transparency, responsibility, performance and information dissemination.

    Our corporate governance is focused on:

    Mitigate any potential conflict of interest.

    Foster a disciplined reporting culture reporting and monitoring.

    Make decisions that maximize the value of the company.

    Improve the interaction between the Board of Directors, the Committees and the Administration.

    Be totally transparent in your operations.

    The solid corporate governance structure and practices aligned with the industry.

    Full disclosure of transactions in appropriate financial reports.

    CONFLICTS OF INTEREST

    The highest governing body of Planigrupo is governed by procedures based on the Securities Market Law with the objective of avoiding and managing conflicts of interest. Independent directors are carefully selected based on their experience, capacity and professional prestige, ensuring that they can perform their duties without compromising the interests of the company. Restrictions are established to avoid the appointment of people with direct links to the company, significant influence or command power, as well as those shareholders who are part of the company's control group. Furthermore, it is considered important to avoid any conflict of interest with clients, suppliers and other parties involved in important transactions. These measures seek to safeguard the integrity and impartiality in the decision-making of Planigrupo's governing body.
