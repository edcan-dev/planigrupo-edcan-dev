    • MATERIALITY AND ESG STRATEGY

    At GM Inmobiliaria we are aware of the impacts we have on the environment and society, as well as the impact we have on all our stakeholders. Therefore, during 2021 we suggest reinforcing our sustainability strategy, conducting our first materiality analysis to guide new initiatives and strengthen existing ones.

    The objective of this exercise was to define what were the material issues for the organization and its interest groups. With this, we hope to be able to optimize resources in strategies that generate the greatest benefit for everyone as well as recognize potential risks in the operation.

    The basis for identifying and selecting the participating interest groups was all those groups that are directly involved in traditional operational processes of the business in accordance with our corporate purpose.
