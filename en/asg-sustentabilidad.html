    We selected 12 potential material themes in the materiality analysis, reflected in the following matrix:

    - X axis: The importance of ESG impacts on the business.
    - Y axis: The influence on the evaluations and decisions of interest groups.

    As a first phase of development, we have begun to work on two of the material themes to reinforce our existing practices.

    At GM Inmobiliaria we understand the importance of seeking links with our interest groups, since that We consider it as a business center and fundamental aspect of sustainability; we have adopted the values of the “International Integrated Reporting Framework”, which establishes that the publication of the Sustainability results should provide insight into the nature and quality of relationships between the interest groups and the company, for this reason it is essential to link with them to understand their expectations, needs and concerns, managing to propose concrete solutions with them, in order to prevent risks and develop opportunities.


    To this end, we have developed various communication tools to establish points of contact directly with them and identify the topics and channels of dialogue that are relevant to them; this way We aspire to improve our relationship with them, as well as our processes and services.


    Our engagement process with interest groups includes three phases:

    IDENTIFICATION

    They are defined through an integrated process, the identification of interest groups, as well as premises and the main drivers to determine Your expectations.

    Priorization

    Through the materiality analysis, all our stakeholders were consulted to specify and prioritize the most relevant material topics.

    Validation

    A través del análisis de materialidad, se consultaron a todos nuestros grupos de interés para especificar y priorizar los temas materiales más relevantes.

    CONFLICTS OF INTEREST

    The highest governing body of GM Inmobiliaria is governed by procedures based on the Market Law of Values with the objective of avoiding and managing conflicts of interest. The independent directors are carefully selected based on their experience, capacity and professional prestige, guaranteeing that they can perform their functions without compromising the interests of the company. Restrictions are established to avoid the designation of people with direct links to the society, significant influence or command power, as well as those shareholders who are part of the group of control of the company. Furthermore, it is considered important to avoid any conflict of interest with customers, suppliers and other parties involved in important transactions. These measures seek safeguard the integrity and impartiality in the decision making of the governing body of GM Inmobiliaria.

    At GM Inmobiliaria we are committed to the sustainable development of communities, companies and countries in which we operate, and to do so we have focused on the Sustainable Development Goals (SDG) of the United Nations.

    END OF POVERTY

    GM Inmobiliaria contributes to this objective by creating job opportunities, providing decent working conditions for employees and suppliers and positively impacting communities local.

    GENDER EQUALITY

    At the company we have adopted policies and procedures to guarantee the same rights and job opportunities for women, thus promoting economic growth and development social.

    DECENT WORK AND ECONOMIC GROWTH

    GM Inmobiliaria contributes to the creation of decent jobs in the communities in which it operates. We operate. All women and men, including young people and people with disabilities, have with attractive working conditions, and we are increasingly getting closer to equal pay for work of equal value. Within this, we protect labor rights and promote an environment of safe and risk-free work for all GM Inmobiliaria workers.

    REDUCTION OF INEQUALITIES

    We provide decent working conditions throughout our entire value chain, especially for disadvantaged groups, redistributing salaries more equally to collaborators, implementing mechanisms to avoid tax evasion and promoting cooperation projects development.

    SUSTAINABLE CITIES AND COMMUNITIES

    At GM Inmobiliaria we promote sustainable infrastructures and processes; preserving heritagecultural and natural; positively impacting the cities and human settlements in which it operates and investing in the protection of people potentially affected by natural disasters.

    PEACE, JUSTICE AND SOLID INSTITUTIONS

    At GM Inmobiliaria we incorporate respect for human rights and transparency processes in the organization, avoiding any form of violence with vulnerable groups. We have our code of ethics, we promote actions regarding transparency and anti-corruption.

    ALLIANCES TO ACHIEVE GOALS

    At GM Inmobiliaria we are participants in the promotion of investments in favor of Mexico, which is why We work with national and international tenants. We promote work among the sector public and private, as well as cooperation with civil society, taking advantage of the experience and strategies for obtaining resources from alliances to promote local economic development.
