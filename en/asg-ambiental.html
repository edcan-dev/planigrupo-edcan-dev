    ENVIRONMENTAL

    At GM Inmobiliaria we are aware that we can make a difference with the creation of environmental value, which is why we will implement various campaigns that promote the circular economy: the reuse, savings and awareness of resources within our plazas and corporate offices, of materials such as paper , cardboard, PET, glass, electronics, and even energy and water.

    • 2023 GOALS
    • |
    • ACTIVITIES

    Implementation of resource optimization campaigns for clients, collaborators and suppliers.

    Optimize energy and water consumption in our properties.

    Provide workshops and talks on environmental awareness to collaborators.

    Strengthen the division of waste according to type.

    Make alliances with various organizations that pursue environmental care to provide financial and/or volunteer support.

    Increase the percentage of Wastewater Treatment Plants on our properties.
